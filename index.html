About the
coffee shop

Our coffee shop is located in the Triple Peaks Library. It features 6
tables and free WiFi.

Our main mission is keeping guests cozy and energized.

Besides that, we try to reduce our environmental impact -- so we
don't sell single-use cups. But you can buy one of our reusable cups!
